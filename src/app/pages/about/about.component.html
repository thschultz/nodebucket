About Us

Gryffindor Industries is company built from the great minds that came from Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. For generations, Gryffindor Industries has worked for the busy working class for both muggles and wizards. They have created automobiles, houses, appliances, and applications to be used for both desktop and mobile.

Gryffindor Industries is proud to announced their latest product, the NodeBucket application. This application allows users to login, create a ToDo list, and move over their items to the Done section. This allows busy muggles and wizards to keep their tasks in order. The application was created using the MEAN tech stack. What does that mean? It means our team created this application using the latest in technology. All information is secure so no one can access your information. The application has contact page with all the information needed to contact us. Enjoy!



Thomas Schultz. Owner and CEO

Our founder, Thomas Schultz, found this company after graduating from Hogwarts. Him and three fellow students agreed to help both wizards and muggles. The founding team always had a fascination towards the muggle world and wanted to help out as much as they could.


Thomas continues to oversee all changes in the company. His goal is to expand his company all over the world.
