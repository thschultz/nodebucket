About Us

Gryffindor Bank and Trust has been a trusted financial institute for the Wizarding Community for over 150 years. We have helped Wizards and Witches get them through Wizarding schools, buy their first house, and starting new businesses. We have always been proud to serve the Wizarding community and are excited to introduce our new Loan App.

Our customers have been asking for a loan application and our developers have delivered. Customers will now be able to calculate a loan without talking to one of our representatives. This will give our customers a good idea how much their monthly payments and interest that will have to be paid will be. Due note, this is not the actual amount you will pay. Interest rates are due to change based on your credit score. Please enjoy our new Loan Application. Please visit the Contact Us page to reach out to someone in our office. Thank and enjoy!



Thomador Longbottom

Our founder, Thomador Longbottom, created Gryffindor Bank & Trust shortly after his tenure at Hogwarts. His goal was to help newly graduated students get the secure loan they needed for a new home. He also helped parents set up financial accounts for their young wizards or witches to prepare them for a great financial future. Thomador is the great-great-great grandfather of famous Gryffindor, Neville Longbottom. His legacy continues today with the Gryffindor Scholarship that helps underprivileged wizards and witches get the help they need to succeed at any Wizarding School.
